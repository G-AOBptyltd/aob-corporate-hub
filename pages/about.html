About Agility Ops

Building smart tools and delivering expert training for agile teams. Australian owned, globally focused.

Our Story

Agility Ops was founded in Australia with a simple mission: bridge the gap between agile theory and practice. We recognized that many teams struggle not with understanding agile principles, but with implementing them effectively in their unique context.

We started by combining hands-on coaching through FACT Training with intelligent tooling. Our founder, Gregory Collocott, brought over a decade of direct agile experience, and together with our team, we built solutions that address real problems teams face every day.

Today, we're proud to support over 500 teams across multiple continents through our three complementary brands: SprintINSite for sprint analytics, PortfolioInSite for portfolio visibility, and FACT Training for hands-on agile coaching.

We remain 100% Australian owned and operated, and we're committed to continuing to build the tools and provide the training that helps teams ship faster and work smarter.
